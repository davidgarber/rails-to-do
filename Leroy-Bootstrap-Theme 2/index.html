About La Popular

At La Popular, we create authentic Mexican food with the original flavor of Jalisco. We preserve the authentic flavor of Mexican cuisine, while promoting our culture and tradition. We use fresh and quality ingredients are used; organic products whenever possible, available, or in season. Our priority is to use products with Eco-Friendly Alternative from local farmers.
